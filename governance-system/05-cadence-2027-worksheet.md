# 05 · 2027 Cadence — Collaborative Planning Worksheet

> **No 2027 dates are assumed here.** Per the explicit direction, 2027 cadence is built *with*
> the committee liaisons. This file is the **worksheet and the guardrails** for that
> conversation, not a proposed schedule. Fill it in during the cadence-setting working session
> (`10-rollout-and-liaison-process.md`). Blank rows live in `data/cadence-2027-worksheet.csv`.

## How to use this
1. Run the **cadence-setting working session** with all Tier-1 liaisons in the room (see `10`).
2. Liaisons set the **Board dates first** (they anchor everything).
3. Each feeder committee then sets its date to satisfy its **confirmed lead time** before the
   downstream meeting (the gaps validated in `02`).
4. Check every proposed date against the **2027 constraint calendar** (below) — closures,
   reduced-capacity days, and the **Deep Work Weeks CACF chooses for 2027**.
5. Record the result in the worksheet + the Meetings list; the automation takes it from there.

## Carry-over decisions from 2026 to settle for 2027
- Whether to **lock Deep Work Weeks onto the board-book build windows** (it worked well in Q2/Q3
  2026 and was the gap in Q4).
- Whether to keep **F&A at Board − 7 calendar days** or move it earlier to fold into Wave 1 of
  the book (the `03` two-wave question).
- Whether **EC's agenda-setting gap** should be standardized (Q4 2026 was tight at ~12 days).
- Confirm **CAC / LIIC / BDC** move from "tentative month" to firm dates with real lead times.
- Bring **Tier-2** committees (BAMA GC, MIB, scholarship) into the worksheet if their liaisons
  want the standardized package.
