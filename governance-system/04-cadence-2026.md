## C. Cross-cutting 2026 observations (for the liaison conversation)
- **Deep Work Weeks are board-book sprints.** Jun 8–11 and Sep 8–10 sit exactly 7 business days
  before their board meetings. Worth *intentionally* reserving future DWWs for board-book builds.
- **F&A = Board − 7 calendar days** every quarter. Combined with a 7-business-day book post, F&A's
  output can't make Wave 1 — hence the two-wave model in `03` (confirm the policy).
- **The thin-margin quarters are Q2 (Juneteenth) and Q4 (Thanksgiving + Winter Reset + no DWW).**
  Q3 has the most breathing room.
